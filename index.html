About Me
I was in 8th grade where in our annual school gathering the horrors of my public speaking came to the forefront. This inability stemmed from not one but multiple reasons. I knew if I had to get over these flaws I'll have to go undergo a self-transformation journey.
Today public speaking, communication skills, being outspoken, self-reflective writing are some of my strong personality traits.
I also am a sports enthusiast, absolutely love connecting to new people. I am always in search of finding the most optimum and effective way of doing even the smallest of things. I am a keen learner and love to work on projects where I get exposed to it's technical as well as commercial side.
